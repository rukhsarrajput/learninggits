Our story

Dream House Plan is a local, reliable home improvements company serving the UK’s geographic regions of Herefordshire, Worcestershire and Gloucestershire. Our services utilise the latest industry products and technologies available, with the aim of protecting and extending the life of your property.

In particular we specialise in the following areas using products backed by manufacturer guarantees:

  • External cleaning & preventative care
  • Specialist roof coatings
  • Specialist wall coatings and colouring
  • Resin bound driveways & paths
  • Restoring & protecting block paving
  • Landscaping

Our team of trained and certified professionals are not only qualified to carry out our specialisations, but also maintain Quantum’s highest standards for attention to detail and exemplary customer service. We believe in complete, 100% customer satisfaction and offer our services with Insurance Backed Guarantees and a No Deposit Requirement.

We understand that making big changes to your home can appear daunting at first, but our highly trained professionals will happily to guide you every step of the way.

Quantum was created with the intention of helping property owners protect and prolong the life of their investments. Whether you are setting up your home for a low-maintenance future, building its value for your dependants, or you just want to get it cleaned up and ship-shape, we can help you realise your needs and offer plenty of no-obligation advice.
